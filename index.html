About Me

Prior to moving to Web Development, I designed logo's and t-shirt designs for athletics clubs around the country. Managing the E-Commerce store and Social Media accounts as well. I've always had a love for the web and coding in general. It is this that pushed me to focus entirely on what I enjoy. Designing and Coding.

I do have a particular love for sports, as such most of the work I've done so far is focused on Running Clubs and Software development for the Garmin Fitness Platform. This is, however, something I am looking to expand on and am excited to take on a wide variety of new projects.
